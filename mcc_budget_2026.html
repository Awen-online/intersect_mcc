About the Committee

🏢 Membership & Community Committee

The MCC connects Intersect to the real world — supporting membership growth, regional onboarding, community programs, and ensuring Intersect membership translates into activity and impact.

Mission

The Membership & Community Committee (MCC) is an Intersect-facing Committee focused on reviewing and improving the membership offerings and membership experience, and growing and supporting participation in the Intersect community. MCC ensures that members are actively engaged by creating pathways for people and organizations to join.

This committee collaborates closely with the Intersect Steering Committee, as both committees have an Intersect-facing focus.

Core Values

Inclusivity
Champion diversity and open governance tooling
Empowerment
Support members to lead impactful initiatives
Transparency
Open dialogue and accessible decision-making
Collaboration
Foster synergy across individuals, teams, and regions
Sustainability
Long-term engagement through responsible planning
Work Packages

MCC 2026 Budget — $300,000 USD

The MCC budget funds seven work packages. Global Community Hubs & Regional Enablement is one component of this broader mandate.

WP 01 · $25,000
Membership Strategy & Growth
Strategy development & research ($10K), KPI dashboard & analytics tooling ($10K), membership framework & documentation ($5K).
WP 02 · $30,000
Member Experience & Engagement
UX improvements for Virtual Hub & onboarding ($10K), engagement campaigns & tooling ($10K), community programs & incentives ($10K).
WP 03 · $26,000
Member Education & Governance Enablement
Content creation — guides & docs ($8K), workshops & sessions ($10K), tooling & delivery platforms ($8K).
WP 04 · $75,000
Global Community Hubs, Regional Enablement & Localization
Hub activity funding pool ($40K), regional coordination & oversight ($10K), performance tracking ($10K), translation services ($8K), contributor incentives ($5K), regional comms ($2K).
WP 05 · $60,000
Community Initiative Enablement
Hackathon execution ($35K), marketing & outreach ($10K), mentorship & incubation ($10K), media & amplification ($5K). Includes Cardano India Hackathon as flagship model.
WP 06 · $50,000
Membership Benefits & Partnerships
Partnership development ($10K), program management ($5K), benefit integration ($5K), Enterprise Member Directory — The Grid ($30K).
WP 07 · $60,000
The Grid Events & Ecosystem Participation
Travel support fund ($20K), Intersect-led events ($15K), local hub events ($15K), branding, swag & NFTs ($10K).
